About Parkinsons Disease Prediction

This Parkinson's disease prediction tool uses a machine learning model trained on biomedical voice measurements. The model analyzes various acoustic features from sustained phonations to predict the likelihood of Parkinson's disease.

Input Parameters:
  • MDVP measurements: Various measures of variation in fundamental frequency
  • Jitter measurements: Several measures of variation in fundamental frequency
  • Shimmer measurements: Several measures of variation in amplitude
  • NHR and HNR: Measures of ratio of noise to tonal components in the voice
  • RPDE: A nonlinear dynamical complexity measure
  • DFA: Signal fractal scaling exponent
  • Spread measures: Nonlinear measures of fundamental frequency variation
  • D2: A nonlinear dynamical complexity measure
  • PPE: A nonlinear measure of fundamental frequency variation
