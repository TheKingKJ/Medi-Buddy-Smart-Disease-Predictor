About Heart Disease Prediction

This heart disease prediction tool uses a machine learning model trained on clinical data. The model analyzes various cardiovascular metrics to predict the likelihood of heart disease.

Input Parameters:
  • Age: Age in years
  • Sex: Gender (1 = male, 0 = female)
  • Chest Pain Type: Type of chest pain
  • Resting Blood Pressure: Resting blood pressure in mm Hg
  • Serum Cholesterol: Serum cholesterol in mg/dl
  • Fasting Blood Sugar: Whether fasting blood sugar > 120 mg/dl
  • Resting ECG Results: Results of resting electrocardiogram
  • Max Heart Rate: Maximum heart rate achieved
  • Exercise Induced Angina: Whether exercise induced angina
  • ST Depression: ST depression induced by exercise relative to rest
  • Slope: The slope of the peak exercise ST segment
  • Major Vessels: Number of major vessels colored by fluoroscopy
  • Thalassemia: A blood disorder called thalassemia
