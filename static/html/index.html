About Medi-buddy

Medi-buddy is an advanced disease prediction system that uses machine learning algorithms to predict the likelihood of various diseases based on patient data. This tool is designed to assist healthcare professionals in early diagnosis and is not intended to replace professional medical advice.

Always consult with a qualified healthcare provider for proper diagnosis and treatment.
